About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! My name is Diego Camilli and I am a developer! I starting developing in early 2022, by self teaching a little bit of Python. IT has been a skill that i have been curious about for a very long time. I have many people in my life who are, in one way or another, developers. I do believe I have the ability to learn and thrive with this new set of skills, therfore I hope put my creative mind to work as well. Eventually i found the UCLA BootCamp where i would start to learn Web Development, and start using technologies like HTML,CSS, and of course JavaScript. Of course it goes without saying that learning everything there is to know in six months is imposible, but the point is to keep learning and grow with the industry.

Something that is very important about me, is that I am disabled. When I was 18, I was diagnosed with Retinitis Pigmentosa. It is a deseise that causes loss of vision. Unfortunatly Retinitis Pigmentosa has many varients and so far there is no cure. However there are many studies that show promise. I say all this because i wish to use my Web Development skills to assist those who also suffur from disabilities. I am saddened by a lot of companies who do not accomidate as well as take into account disabled people. I have ecperienced this first hand.

So my goal as a Web Developer, is to use my knowledge, both as a Developer and as a disabled person, to create and improve applications to better accomidate everyone! Because everyone deserves the ability to have access to our technologies and be independant.
